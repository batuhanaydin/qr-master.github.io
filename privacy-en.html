Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

QR Master does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties.

Overview

QR Master is committed to protecting your privacy at the highest level. This privacy policy explains what data our application collects, uses, and shares.

Data Collection

QR Master application:

  • Does NOT collect your personal information
  • Does NOT require account creation
  • Does NOT send your data to our servers
  • Does NOT use third-party analytics services
  • Does NOT share information with advertising networks

Local Storage

QR Master stores the following data locally on YOUR DEVICE only:

  1. QR Code History: History of QR codes you've created and scanned
  2. App Settings: Your preferences and customizations

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted over the internet
  • Can be deleted from the app at any time
  • Is completely removed when you uninstall the app

Camera Permission

QR Master requests camera access to scan QR codes:

  • Camera is only active when you use the QR code scanning feature
  • Images are not recorded or stored
  • No photos or videos are taken
  • Camera data never leaves your device

Third-Party Services

QR Master does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

QR Master is safe for all age groups. Since we don't collect any personal data, children can safely use the app.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, the security of your data is entirely under your control. All data is stored locally on your device and encrypted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When any changes are made, the new policy will be published on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be changed.
